Top 3 Destinations

Greece

Greece is famous for its crystal clear beaches, lovely architecture and of course, yummy Mediterranean cuisine! Plan and book your holiday to Greece using our website. It’s always sunny and you can reach the sea whenever you want in this beautiful country. Choose Athens, the capital city for stunning history and architecture. If you are more of an island person, choose among thousands of different islands including Kos, Mykonos, Crete, Santorini, Zakynthos, and many more.

Curacao

An island which belongs to the Royal Dutch Kingdom, Curacao is a natural paradise with amazing hospitality and numerous beautiful places to move around off the beaten track. It’s very easy to get there with direct flights via KLM every day from Amsterdam. Also, no immigration hassles if you are from the Netherlands. Whether you stroll down Handelskade, the ancient waterfront strip, or try out new condiments at any recommended food truck, or discover a new route by hiking through Christoffel Park, Curacao will never cease to amaze you.

Norway

Do you want to sail in the Nowegian fjords or go kayaking or playboating in the summer? Or rather go skiing or snowboarding in the raw Scandinavian Mountains? Maybe you dislike sports, but just like to see the city and nature instead? Norway has everything to offer. Northern Lights in the winter, untouched nature, lush green mountains such as the Trolltunga in the summer, and beautiful fjords in Bergen and surrounding areas are few of all the exciting stuff Norway has to offer.
